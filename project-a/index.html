Gargantua

Scientific Name:

Sagittarius B

Discovered by / at:

Dr Kash, 2156

Family:

Gargantua belongs to the family of super massive blackholes of the observable universe.

Habitat:

It is the central object of a planetary system in a distant galaxy.

Appearance:

In its stable habitat, it is radiating yellow and orange light which, due to its strong gravity, appear around its event horizon in a spiral. However, when interacted, the appearance changes to angry red - so stay away

Key Characteristics:

  • Loves feeding on Stars
  • Hates any form of Interaction
  • Very easy to make angry

What we know so far about Gargantua? Gargantua has approximately 100 million times the mass of our Sun, which means it is about 25 times more massive than Sagittarius A*, the supermassive black hole at the center of our Milky Way. It feeds on all kinds of space matter that comes in its way, particularly the big stars which are its favorite food.

Guidelines for human interaction with this creature? For safety, it is advised to not to interact with Gargantua. Currently, in its stable environment, it feeds slowly on space matter. However, if interacted, it gets extremely angry, and starts consuming matter at a very fast speed.
